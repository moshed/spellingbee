How to play

  • Build words from the seven letters.
  • Every word must contain the center letter.
  • Words are at least 4 letters; letters may repeat.
  • Scoring: a 4-letter word = 1 point, then +1 for each extra letter (5 letters = 2, 6 = 3, …). A pangram (uses all 7 letters) earns an extra +7.
  • Play together: tap Invite / Share and send the link. Everyone plays the same board and the scoreboard shows each player's score and when they last updated — great for playing at the same time or whenever you each get a chance.
